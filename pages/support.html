how to play

Hello Skateboarder! Welcome to Skate Rush. I am Alexia, your digital guide here to provide you with all the necessary guidance to navigate the world of skateboarding.

In Easy mode, players will encounter various obstacles, requiring players to jump and avoid them to maintain their four lives. Successful jumps also contribute to the player's score, adding to the excitement of the game.

In Difficult mode, an alien ship will periodically fire bullets, adding an extra layer of complexity. Players must navigate the obstacles while dodging the alien's projectiles to safeguard their score. Stay sharp and agile to conquer the challenges of Difficult mode!"

easy mode

Player will either have to press the spacebar or left click on their mouse to jump

hard mode

Left-handed players will have to press A keyword and right-handed players will have to press L keyword to block bullets from passing alien ship
